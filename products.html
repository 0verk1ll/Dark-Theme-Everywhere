Devices

The Boox Max 2 is an Android 6.0 tablet with an E Ink display. The Max 2 can also be used as a computer monitor via HDMI.

The Cervantes 3 (page in Spanish) is an ereader with an operating system licensed under GPL. This device is available from Spain.

The Dasung Paperlike is an E Ink monitor that uses HDMI to connect to your computer. It is compatible with Windows and GNU/Linux.

The Game Boy and original Game Boy Advance have screens use ambient light instead of a backlight. With flash cartriges like an Everdrive, modern games developed for these consoles can be played from an SD card or USB drive.

The Light Phone II is a minimalist phone with an E Ink display. This phone intentionally lacks many of the features of a smartphone so you can live without constant distractions.

Glasses

Theraspecs sells glasses designed to reduce frequencies of light that harm people who suffer from migraines.

Gunnar sells computer glasses designed to reduce blue light exposure.

Screen Filters

3M makes screen filters to reduce glare and protect your privacy.
